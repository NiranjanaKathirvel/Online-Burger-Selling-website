Menu

Classic Burger

A simple burger made with a ground beef patty, usually cooked on a grill or stovetop served on a soft bun, and topped with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.

Cheese Burger

Similar to a classic hamburger, but with the addition of cheese, which is often melted on top of the patty.

Veg Burger

A vegetarian or vegan alternative to meat-based burgers, veggie burgers are made from a variety of plant-based ingredients like beans, lentils.

BBQ Burger

The BBQ burger is a delicious combination of a perfectly grilled beef patty, smothered in tangy barbecue sauce and topped with caramelized onions and melted cheese.

Chicken Burger

Instead of beef, chicken burgers use a ground or marinated chicken patty. They can be grilled, fried, or breaded and served with lettuce, tomato, onion.

Fish Burger

Fish burgers are typically made with a breaded or grilled fish fillet. Common fish options include cod, salmon, or tuna.
